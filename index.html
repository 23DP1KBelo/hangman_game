Hangman!

Rules

Gameplay:

A random word is selected, and the number of letters is shown.
Type a guess (same length as the word) or letters and press Enter.

Feedback:

Correct letters in the right position are shown.
Incorrect letters are displayed as underscores (_).
Letters in the word but in the wrong position are listed separately.

Used Letters:

A list of all guessed letters will be shown.
The letters that are used and are in the word will be shown below the guess.

Attempts:

You have 6 guesses. After 6 wrong guesses, the game ends.

Win Condition:

Guess the word correctly before running out of attempts.

Game Over:

After 6 incorrect guesses, the correct word will be shown.

Play Again:

Click Play Again to start a new round.
